Our story

Lava Cafe opened in october 2005. The owner Jerry Hogan, who is a lover of music, coffee, healthy smoothies drinks, vegetarian food, antiques, vintage records and more desired to combine all this elements together and create a unique place in the famous London's Bond street. After two years of all the design, inspections and constructions Lava cafe finally opened in october.

Our goal is very simple. We wish to Inspire the appreciation of excellent coffee and meaningful relationships. Everyday is a chance to enjoy life with others and we hope to be a part of yours. We believe that the best way to do business is to do it with excellence. From our coffee to design, our approach is to create what we love and we think you will love it too. We are inviting you to join and be a part of our story.

We are open every day.If you want to make a reservation you can contact as on lavacafe@example.com or via contact form bellow. Please notify us 24 hours in advance if you need to reschedule as we understand that unexpected emergencies come up.
